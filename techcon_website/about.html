Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small developer meetup and has grown into a global conference bringing together the brightest minds in technology. Over the years, we've hosted thousands of attendees, hundreds of talks, and industry-shaping product announcements.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, share knowledge, and build a stronger global tech community. We believe in creating a platform that is inclusive, forward-thinking, and driven by curiosity and collaboration.
