Welcome to TechCon 2024

TechCon 2024 is the ultimate tech conference bringing together innovators, developers, and industry leaders to share insights and shape the future of technology.

History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small meetup of developers and tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a global event attracting thousands from around the world.

Mission

Our mission is to empower the next generation of technology leaders through inspiring talks, hands-on workshops, and a vibrant community.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Lisa Tan

Renowned AI researcher and advocate for ethical technology. She spoke about the future of human-AI collaboration in 2022.

Mark Chen

Cybersecurity expert who led a keynote on digital safety in 2020. He is known for innovations in encryption and data privacy.

Priya Singh

Founder of GreenTech Startups. She inspired attendees with her journey and how tech can help fight climate change.
